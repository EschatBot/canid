What it does

Canid uses your phone's camera and microphone to read your dog's behavior in real time. It continuously classifies what your dog is expressing — combining posture analysis, motion detection, and vocalization recognition into a unified intelligence layer. No guessing, no cloud, no account. Just understanding, instantly, on your device.

Behavioral states

Eight windows into
your dog's world.

🎾

Playful

Energetic movement, play bow posture, high-pitched vocalizations

Anxious

Panting, pacing, lowered tail, excessive self-grooming

Alert

Ears forward, rigid posture, focused attention on a stimulus

Fearful

Tucked tail, cowering, trembling, avoidance behavior

Relaxed

Soft eyes, loose body language, slow breathing, resting posture

Wants Attention

Pawing, nudging, soft whining, sustained eye contact with handler

Frustrated

Persistent barking, repetitive behavior, stiff tail carriage

Excited

Spinning, jumping, whole-body wag, high-energy vocalization

Privacy

Everything stays on
your phone.

This is not a feature. It's a commitment. Your dog's data — their voice, their image, their behavior — belongs to you and only you. Always.

All inference runs on-device

Neural networks run locally. No video, audio, or behavioral data ever leaves your phone.

No internet connection required

Works completely offline. No Wi-Fi, no cellular, no server calls of any kind.

Zero accounts or sign-ups

Open the app, point the camera, see results. No email, no password, no profile created.

Open source and fully auditable

Every line of code is public on GitHub. Verify the privacy claims yourself.
