Canid 🐕

A Dog Behavioral Translator

What it does

Canid uses your phone's camera and microphone to read your dog's behavior in real time. It continuously classifies what your dog is expressing across 8 behavioral states:

🎾 Playful 😟 Anxious 👁️ Alert 😨 Fearful 😴 Relaxed 🐾 Wants Attention 😤 Frustrated ⚡ Excited

Classification is based on peer-reviewed animal behavior research — combining body posture, motion, and vocalization cues to give you an accurate picture of your dog's emotional state.

🔒 All on-device 📵 No data leaves your phone 🚫 No account required 👁️ No tracking
